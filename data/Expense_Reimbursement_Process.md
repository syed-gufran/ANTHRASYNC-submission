# Acme Corp — Expense Reimbursement Process

## 1. Eligible Expenses
Employees may claim reimbursement for business travel, client meals, approved
software subscriptions, and home-office equipment. **Personal expenses are not
reimbursable.**

## 2. Daily Limits
- **Meals:** up to $50 per day during domestic travel, $75 per day international.
- **Hotel:** up to $200 per night domestic, $300 per night international.
- **Local transport:** actual cost, receipts required above $25.

## 3. Submission Deadline
Expense claims must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense being
incurred. Claims older than 30 days require Finance Director approval and are
considered case by case.

## 4. Required Documentation
Every claim must include an **itemized receipt**. Credit-card statements alone
are not accepted. For expenses above **$500**, prior written approval from the
employee's manager is mandatory.

## 5. Approval & Payment
Submitted claims are approved by the line manager, then processed by Finance.
Reimbursements are paid out with the **next payroll cycle**, provided the claim
is approved at least 5 business days before payroll runs.
