# Acme Corp — Customer Refund & Returns Policy

## 1. Refund Window
Customers may request a **full refund within 30 days** of purchase, provided the
product is unused and in its original packaging. After 30 days, refunds are not
available, but store credit may be issued at the company's discretion up to day 60.

## 2. Refund Processing Time
Approved refunds are processed within **5 to 7 business days** and credited to
the original payment method. Refunds to bank transfers may take up to 10 business
days depending on the bank.

## 3. Non-Refundable Items
The following are **non-refundable**: gift cards, downloadable software,
and personalized/custom-made items. Subscription services can be cancelled any
time but are non-refundable for the current billing cycle.

## 4. Damaged or Defective Products
If a product arrives damaged or defective, customers must report it within
**48 hours** of delivery with photographic evidence. Acme Corp will arrange a
free replacement or a full refund, including shipping charges.

## 5. How to Request a Refund
Refund requests are submitted through the customer portal under
**Orders → Request Refund**, or by emailing support@acme.example. A support
agent responds within 1 business day.
